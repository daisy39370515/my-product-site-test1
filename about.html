Our Story

Founded in 2010, we have grown from a small lighting workshop to a leading solar lighting manufacturer with a global presence. Our journey has been driven by a passion for sustainable lighting solutions and a commitment to excellence in every product we create.

Over the years, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner for businesses worldwide, delivering high-quality solar lighting products that meet international standards and exceed customer expectations.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities span over 10,000 square meters, equipped with advanced production lines and quality control systems that ensure every product meets our rigorous standards.

Our Mission

To provide innovative, reliable, and sustainable solar lighting solutions that empower businesses and communities to reduce energy costs while contributing to a greener planet.

We believe in the power of solar technology to transform the way the world is illuminated. Our mission extends beyond simply selling products; we aim to be a catalyst for positive change in the global lighting industry.

Through continuous innovation, we strive to develop cutting-edge solar lighting solutions that combine efficiency, durability, and aesthetics to meet the evolving needs of our clients.

Our Vision

To become the global leader in solar lighting solutions, setting industry standards for quality, innovation, and customer service while driving the transition to sustainable energy worldwide.

We envision a future where solar lighting is the preferred choice for outdoor illumination across all sectors, from residential areas to industrial complexes, from city streets to remote villages.

Our vision is built on three pillars: technological innovation, environmental responsibility, and customer-centric service. By excelling in these areas, we aim to shape the future of the solar lighting industry.
